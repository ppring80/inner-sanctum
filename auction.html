Draft Strategy

Choose your approach. The Oracle will adjust your target prices to match your game plan.

🏃 RB Heavy
Load up on elite running backs early. Spend big on two RB1s and fill the rest cheap.
Best for: PPR leagues with strong RB ADP
🎯 WR Heavy
Target elite receivers. Let others overpay for RBs while you secure top pass catchers.
Best for: PPR leagues, receiver-friendly offenses
🎳 QB Heavy
Secure an elite QB early. In superflex or 2QB leagues this is often mandatory.
Best for: Superflex / 2QB leagues
🦸 Hero RB
One elite RB1 anchor, then pivot to WRs. Balance studs with consistent contributors.
Best for: Balanced leagues, first-time auction drafters
